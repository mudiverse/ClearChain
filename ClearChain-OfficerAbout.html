Clear Chain

ClearChain is an innovative anti-corruption platform that leverages blockchain, smart contracts, and citizen engagement to ensure transparency and accountability in public procurement and fund disbursement processes. Using a Flutter-based mobile app, citizens can anonymously report irregularities with supporting evidence, such as images and geolocation data. Reports are securely logged on a blockchain, creating an immutable audit trail. A Node.js backend processes data and employs real-time anomaly detection to flag suspicious patterns.
